About Us

Here's everything you need to know about us.

Bridge Quality Education Africa is an organization made up of communities of volunteers whose aim is to improve education in Africa to allow students contribute to community developmnent in their localities


Vision Statement

To build a volunteering network in Africa that equips students with quality education practices and life skills that make them ready to build their dreams and build their communities


Mission Statement

To provide modern educational practices and tools to equip students with life skills by organizing traning sessions and volunteering projects for students to acquire and practice these skills


Values

    Service

    Integrity

    Commitment

    Respect

    Team work
